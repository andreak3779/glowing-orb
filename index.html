My philosophy on software development

In todays software developement world change is constant. We change our tools, we change our apps, we change our environment, and our jobs. New jobs are created old jobs are eliminated. Although one thing has stayed constant for me my personal philosophy on my work in the software development world. It is to let the tool do the work so I don't have to. Simple statement but what does it mean? It means to me these points.

  • Automate repetitive tasks as much as reasonable possible.
  • Put effort into mastering your development environment
  • Always be learning about different tools, languages, OS etc...
  • Understand the intention of the tools, languages and environment that your are working within.
  • Always try to find ways that your current environment can be improved.
  • Share any information, tools or skills you have learned with your team members and ask for feedback.
